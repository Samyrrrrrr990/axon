# What is Axon?

Axon is a local-first visual platform for building real AI. Instead of writing
code, you drag nodes onto a canvas and wire them together: load a dataset, split
it, train a model, evaluate it, chart the results. The same canvas also covers
fine-tuning pretrained language models with LoRA and composing LLM pipelines
with retrieval and agents.

Everything runs on your own machine. Your data never leaves your computer
unless you explicitly connect a cloud model provider.
